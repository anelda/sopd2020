Many surveys related to open science, open data, and open access have been developed over the years. We've been sorting and comparing the questions and are developing a databank of pre-existing survey questions that can be used for future surveys about data sharing practices. A Google Sheet is available online where you can view the process of comparison. This is a work in progress - we're trying to be as transparent as possible throughout the research process.
